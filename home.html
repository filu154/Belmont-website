Our Home

Belmont Villa is a picturesque Victorian building with a ground-floor extension designed specifically for easy access and wheelchairs.

It is built on three levels, is tastefully decorated with period features and is fully carpeted and centrally heated. We have four lounges with televisions and a conservatory reserved for quiet relaxation and socialising. Also three large dining areas.

Communal dining is a good practice so we encourage people to eat in the dining rooms but arrangements can be made for people who prefer to eat privately.
